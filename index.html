    Riftward controls

    WASDMove one tile. Arrow keys work too.

    1Shoot the nearest visible enemy. Space and F also fire.

    2Use an Echo pulse. Q is the alternate key.

    3Use a field patch. H is the alternate key.

    4Reload the current weapon. R is the alternate key.

    GLoad the next floor from anywhere after the sector is clear. E also works.

    BOpen the field shop to buy or equip weapons and supplies.

    TABOpen the persistent score board and run statistics.

    MToggle automatic route mode.

    XWait one turn without moving.

    CLICKMove toward a visible tile, shoot an enemy, or activate the gold gate.

    ESCClose the shop, score board, or controls.

    Every combat action is one turn. Kills award score and persistent Vault Tokens. A cleared sector never requires walking back to the gate: press G or use NEXT FLOOR.
